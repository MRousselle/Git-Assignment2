The Carnival of Time is the day at the very end of the Termina calendar where we celebrate the year gone by and commit it to our bygone memories. The Clock Tower opens and we cheer to the sound of the twelve chimes of midnight. There will be ceremonies to celebrate the Moon as well as offerings from all of Termina's native races.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cost to attend the Carnival of Time

The Carnival is completely free! We welcome you to bring rupees for games and Masks, but food and entertainment is free for you to enjoy.

What do I need to bring with me to the Carnival?

Bring a mask along with you to help ring in the new year and celebrate with family and friends!

What if I don't own a mask?

If you don't have a mask, feel free to make one or buy one at the Carnival! IF you want to purchase one beforehandplease visit this page.

Where can I get more information about sending a mask for an offering?

Please visit our page for long-distance offerings.

At the Carnival, how can I stand out?

Be yourself! Be loud and exciting! Where a msk that represents who you are, and people will be sure to love having you!
